Biography

I obtained my PhD degree at the Department of Computer Science, Kent State University in December, 2021, under the co-supervision of Dr. Xiang Lian and Dr. Kambiz Ghazinour. Starting from February 2022, I am a research scientist at Shenzhen Institute of Computing Sciences.
